Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost for mulch installation?

The cost of mulching services can vary depending on whether companies charge a flat rate or by the hour. On average, labor costs for mulching range from $55 to $90 per hour, equivalent to $20 to $45 per cubic yard. This means you can typically expect to pay around $50 to $155 per cubic yard for mulching, covering both materials and labor. However, actual prices may differ based on factors like the chosen company's pricing model, the type of mulch used, and any additional services requested.

What is the reccomended depth for mulch installation?

As beneficial as mulch is, too much can be harmful. The generally recommended mulching depth is 2 to 4 inches. We usually reccomend 3 inches for an avergae mulching job.

Is there a minimim of hours that need to be hired for our services?

To secure your project on our schedule, we do require a minimum booking of 3 hours. Please note that full lawn care maintenance and pressure washing services are exceptions to this requirement. This policy is in place to ensure effective scheduling and resource allocation for all projects. Thank you for your cooperation.

Do you provide mulch deliveries?

We can deliver up to 5 to 6 yards of mulch. For larger quantities exceeding this amount, we recommend engaging a third-party mulch company equipped with larger trucks capable of handling 7 plus yards. These external providers often offer more competitive rates for deliveries exceeding 7 yards, ensuring you receive the best value for your mulch requirements. Thank you for considering our services.

Do you offer haul away services for yard debris or any other miscellaneous items?

Yes, we do provide haul away services, typically priced by the truck load. A single full truck load is $150, and for two truck loads, the rate is $250.

What payment methods does College Yard Pro accept?

We accept all kinds of payments like check, venmo, zelle, debit/credit card. You can expect to make the payment after all of the work is done and everything is in order.
